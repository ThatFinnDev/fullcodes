What even is F-Droid?

F-Droid is basically a third party PlayStore. You can add your own sources
and download apps from within the app like the PlayStore.

 

What can't I just download Fullcodes apps from the PlayStore?

You can, but F-Droid is better. Firstly, not all of our apps are available on
the PlayStore. Secondly, game updates are earlier accessible on F-Droid
because Google scans every game update (which takes a long time). Thirdly,
you can install older versions of games if you want to.

 

This guide will help you add the Fullcodes repository to F-Droid!
